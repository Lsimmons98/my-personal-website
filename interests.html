Interests

Scuba Diving

Scuba diving has been a cherished hobby of mine, offering unforgettable experiences beneath the waves. This January, I achieved my advanced certification during a remarkable journey aboard a liveaboard boat in the British Virgin Islands. The opportunity to explore the vibrant underwater world in such a breathtaking setting was truly unparalleled. What makes this hobby even more special is sharing it with my mom, who has been an avid diver for as long as I can remember. Together, we've delved into the depths of the oceans, from the coastal waters of New York to the tropical paradises of the Bahamas and Florida. Each dive is a new adventure, filled with awe-inspiring marine life and the serenity of the ocean's embrace.

Fishkeeping

Maintaining home aquariums has been a long-standing passion of mine, cultivated since before I even embarked on my scuba diving adventures. Creating and caring for underwater ecosystems has provided me with a sense of tranquility and fascination, allowing me to observe the intricate dynamics of aquatic life from the comfort of my own home. Each aquarium becomes a miniature world unto itself, offering a glimpse into the beauty and complexity of the underwater realm.

Motorcycle Riding

Motorcycle riding is another passion of mine, allowing me to experience the freedom of the open road and connect with nature. Whether cruising along scenic routes or navigating through urban streets, every ride is an exhilarating journey. Exploring new destinations and discovering hidden gems on two wheels brings a sense of adventure and excitement to my life.
